Slug FAQ

What are stop words?
Stop words are common words like "and", "the", "in", "of" that add little SEO value. Trimming them makes your slugs shorter and more focused on primary keywords.
Why use hyphens instead of underscores?
Google and other search engines treat hyphens as word separators, while underscores are often seen as part of a single word. Hyphens are generally better for SEO.

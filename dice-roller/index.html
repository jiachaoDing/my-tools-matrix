Dice Roller FAQ

What dice types do you support?
We support standard polyhedral dice including 4-sided (D4), 6-sided (D6), 8-sided (D8), 10-sided (D10), 12-sided (D12), and 20-sided (D20).
How is the total calculated?
The total is simply the sum of all visible faces after the dice finish rolling. We also keep a session-based history so you can see previous sums.
Is the 3D animation accurate?
For the classic D6, we show a full 3D rotating cube. For other polyhedral dice (D4, D20, etc.), we use a high-performance 2D-on-3D representation to ensure compatibility and speed across all devices while maintaining the immersive "roll" feel.

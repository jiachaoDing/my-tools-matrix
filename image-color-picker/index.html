Color Picker FAQ

Does it work with SVG files?
Yes, you can upload SVG files. They will be rasterized to a canvas locally so you can pick colors from them just like a regular photo.
Is there a maximum image size?
There is no strict limit, but extremely large images (e.g., over 50MB) may perform slower depending on your computer's hardware. Most standard photos will work instantly.
Are my images saved?
No. Your images stay entirely within your browser's memory and are discarded as soon as you close the page or remove the image. We never upload your files to our servers.

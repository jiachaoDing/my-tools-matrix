Expert Q&A

What is "Natural Sorting"?
Standard alphabetical sorting treats strings character-by-character, meaning "File 10" comes before "File 2". Natural sorting handles multi-digit numbers as single values, placing "File 2" before "File 10", which is how humans expect to see them.
Can it handle thousands of lines?
Yes. Our engine is highly optimized for performance and can process large lists efficiently using your browser's local resources. The only limit is your device's available memory.
Is my list data secure?
100%. All sorting happens entirely within your browser's memory. No data is ever sent to our servers or stored anywhere. Your lists remain completely private.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why convert WebP to PNG?
PNG is universally supported across all platforms and software, making it ideal for maximum compatibility. PNG also offers lossless compression and full transparency support, perfect for logos, graphics, and images with text.
Is transparency preserved in PNG conversion?
Yes! PNG format fully supports alpha transparency, so any transparent areas in your WebP images will be perfectly preserved in the converted PNG files. This makes PNG ideal for logos and graphics.
Are my images safe and private?
Absolutely! This tool processes everything locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images are never uploaded to any server or cloud service. No data leaves your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.
Can I convert multiple WebP files at once?
Yes! This tool supports unlimited batch processing. Select as many WebP files as you need, and they'll all be converted to PNG simultaneously. You can then download them individually or all at once.
Will the PNG files be larger than WebP?
Generally yes, PNG files are larger than WebP because WebP uses superior compression. However, PNG offers universal compatibility and lossless quality. The file size increase is the tradeoff for better compatibility and guaranteed quality preservation.

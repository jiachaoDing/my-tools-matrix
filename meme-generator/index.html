Meme Generator FAQ

Are my memes private?
Yes. Unlike other generators that upload your images to their servers, our tool works entirely in your browser. Your images and text never leave your device.
Can I use my own images?
Absolutely! Click the upload box or drag any image file from your computer to use it as a custom background.
Is there a limit to the text length?
There is no character limit, but the font size will stay consistent. If you have a lot of text, we recommend reducing the font size using the slider.

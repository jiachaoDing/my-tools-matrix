Expert Q&A

What is "Sentence case"?
Sentence case capitalizes only the first letter of each sentence, making it ideal for large blocks of descriptive text. Our tool intelligently identifies sentence endings to ensure correct capitalization.
How does "Title Case" differ?
Title Case capitalizes the first letter of every word (excluding some small joining words in complex versions). It's most commonly used for book titles, blog headers, and professional document titles.

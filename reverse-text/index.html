Expert Q&A

What is the difference between Reverse Text and Reverse Lines?
Reverse Text flips the entire string character by character (e.g., "abc" becomes "cba"). Reverse Lines keeps the content of each line intact but flips the order of the lines (e.g., Line 1, Line 2 becomes Line 2, Line 1).
Can I process multiple files at once?
Yes. You can upload multiple text files, choose your reversal mode, and the tool will process all of them. You can then download all the reversed files as a batch.

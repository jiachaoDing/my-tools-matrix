Expert Q&A

Is there a limit on the file size?
Technically, there is no hard limit. However, since all processing happens in your browser's RAM, merging very large files (e.g., hundreds of megabytes) will depend on your device's available memory.
Can I merge password-protected PDFs?
No, encrypted or password-protected files cannot be merged for security reasons. You should remove the password from your documents before attempting to merge them.
Will my links and bookmarks stay intact?
Most hyperlinks within pages are preserved. However, global bookmarks (outlines) from the individual documents may not be automatically merged into a single outline in the final output file.

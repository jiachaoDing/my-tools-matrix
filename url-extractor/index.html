Frequently Asked Questions

What protocol types are supported?
Our extractor identifies http, https, ftp, and www links. It works across HTML source code, CSS files, and plain text.
Is it safe to paste confidential code?
Yes. The entire extraction process happens within your browser's memory. No data is sent to our servers, ensuring your source code remains strictly confidential.
What tracking parameters are removed?
When "Clean Tracking Params" is enabled, the tool strips common identifiers such as utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, fbclid, gclid, and other similar analytics markers.

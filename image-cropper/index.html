Common Questions

Will my image lose quality?
No. We crop the image at its original resolution. If you choose PNG or lossless formats, there is zero quality loss. For JPEG, we use a high-quality setting to ensure sharpness.
What formats are supported?
We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP for both input and output.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes! Our cropper is fully responsive and supports touch events, making it easy to crop photos on your smartphone or tablet with natural gestures.

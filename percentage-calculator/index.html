Percentage FAQ

How is percentage change calculated?
Percentage change is calculated by taking the difference between the final and initial values, dividing by the absolute value of the initial value, and multiplying by 100. Formula: `((Final - Initial) / Initial) * 100`.
What if the percentage is over 100%?
Our calculator handles percentages of any size. For example, 200% of a value simply means double that value, and 500% means five times that value.

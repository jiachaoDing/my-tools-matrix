Expert Q&A

When should I measure?
For consistency, measure in the morning after waking up and using the bathroom, but before eating. Your body water content changes throughout the day, which can affect measurements slightly.
How accurate is this method?
While no formula is perfect, the Navy method is widely considered the most accurate "tape measurement" method. It typically stays within 3-4% of expensive DEXA scan results, making it an excellent tool for tracking progress over time.

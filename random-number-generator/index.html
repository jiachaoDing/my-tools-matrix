Random Number FAQ

Are these numbers truly random?
Yes. We use the crypto.getRandomValues() method, which provides cryptographically strong pseudo-random numbers. This is much more secure than the standard Math.random() used by most other websites.
Is there a limit to how many numbers I can generate?
Currently, you can generate up to 1,000 numbers at once. This ensures the browser remains responsive while giving you plenty of data for most use cases.
Can I use this offline?
Absolutely! Once the page is loaded, all calculations happen on your device. You don't need an active internet connection to generate numbers.

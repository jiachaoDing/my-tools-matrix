Frequently Asked Questions

Does it account for leap years?
Yes. The calculator utilizes standard chronological libraries that correctly account for February 29th and the varying lengths of all months throughout the year.
Why "Include last day"?
Standard date subtraction usually counts the sleep/night gaps between days. If you are tracking a 3-day event (e.g. Friday to Sunday), you might want to "Include last day" to count all three full days instead of just the 2-day gap.

QR Generator FAQ

Will my QR code work with a logo?
Yes! QR codes have built-in error correction. By setting the error level to "High", you can cover up to 30% of the QR code with a logo and it will still be scanable.
Do these QR codes expire?
No, these are "Static QR Codes". The information is encoded directly in the pattern, so they will work forever as long as the content (like a URL) is valid.

IP Address FAQ

What is an IP address?
An IP (Internet Protocol) address is a unique numerical label assigned to each device connected to a computer network that uses the Internet Protocol for communication.
How accurate is the location?
IP-based geolocation is accurate to the city or region level but rarely to a specific street address. It depends on how your ISP registers their IP blocks.

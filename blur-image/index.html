Photo Blur FAQ

What is the best blur level for privacy?
To completely hide faces or sensitive text, we recommend a blur radius of at least 25-30px. For aesthetic background blurs, 5-15px is usually ideal.
Does it support high-resolution photos?
Yes! Our tool processes images at their original resolution. For extremely large files (e.g., 40MP+), there might be a short delay during the initial processing depending on your device's memory.
Is this tool free to use?
Yes, it is 100% free with no hidden costs, limits, or watermarks. We believe basic utility tools should be accessible to everyone.

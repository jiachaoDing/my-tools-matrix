Expert Q&A

Will this work on Instagram or Twitter?
Yes! Since these are Unicode characters and not actual fonts, they are supported by almost all social media platforms and messaging apps. They are perfect for bios and usernames.
Why are some characters missing?
The Unicode standard doesn't have a small version for every single character. For example, some letters in subscript are not defined. Our generator uses the closest available mappings to ensure the best possible look.
Can I process text files?
Absolutely. You can drag and drop `.txt` files into the input zone. The tool will convert them into small caps and allow you to download the results individually or as a batch.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are MP4 files smaller than GIFs?
GIF uses lossless compression designed for simple graphics, storing each frame individually. MP4 uses advanced video codecs (H.264) that compress efficiently by storing only changes between frames. This results in files that are 80-90% smaller while maintaining better visual quality.
Is my data secure during conversion?
Absolutely! All conversion happens locally in your browser using FFmpeg compiled to WebAssembly. Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security.
Will the MP4 play on social media?
Yes! Our converter outputs MP4 files with H.264 encoding, which is universally supported by all major platforms including Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, Discord, WhatsApp, and more. The files are optimized for web playback.
Why does the first conversion take longer?
The first conversion requires loading FFmpeg.wasm (~30MB), which is a one-time download. Once loaded, it's cached by your browser, making subsequent conversions much faster. This is the trade-off for complete privacy.

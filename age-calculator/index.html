Frequently Asked Questions

How is leap year handled?
Our calculator uses the built-in JavaScript Date object, which natively handles leap years and varying month lengths (28, 29, 30, or 31 days) automatically.
Is this accurate for travel across timezones?
Chronological age is typically calculated based on the local time of your current location. This tool uses your system's current timezone to ensure consistency with your local calendar.

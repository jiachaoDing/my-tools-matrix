Frequently Asked Questions

Why convert BMP to JPG?
BMP files are uncompressed and can be extremely large. Converting to JPG reduces file size by up to 90% while maintaining excellent visual quality, making images perfect for web use, email attachments, and storage optimization.
Is this tool completely free?
Yes! Our BMP to JPG converter is 100% free with no hidden fees, registration requirements, or file limits. Convert as many images as you need, whenever you need.
What quality setting should I use?
For web use, 85% quality offers the best balance between file size and visual quality. Use 90-95% for print materials or professional photography. Lower settings (50-70%) are ideal for thumbnails or when storage space is critical.
Are my images safe?
Absolutely! All conversions are performed locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your BMP files never get uploaded to any server, ensuring your data remains completely private and secure.

Emoji FAQ

What are Unicode Emojis?
Emojis are part of the Unicode Standard, which means they are assigned specific code points. This ensures they look consistent across different devices and operating systems, though the visual design may vary.
Are these emojis free to use?
Yes! Emojis are a standard feature of modern systems. You can use them in any text field that supports Unicode, which includes almost all modern websites and apps.

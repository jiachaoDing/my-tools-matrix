Tax FAQ

What is the difference between Net and Gross?
Net Price is the value before any tax is added. Gross Price is the final amount including tax. In "Remove Tax" mode, you start with Gross and find the Net.
Is it "Reverse Sales Tax"?
Yes. The "Remove Tax" mode is often called reverse tax calculation. It uses the formula: `Net = Gross / (1 + Rate)`. This ensures that when you add the tax back to the Net, you get the exact Gross value again.

Watermark Maker FAQ

Will my original images be overwritten?
No. This tool creates new copies of your images with the watermark added. Your original files remain untouched on your device.
What is the best position for a watermark?
Standard placement is often the bottom-right corner. However, for maximum protection against unauthorized cropping, a centered watermark with low opacity is recommended.
Are my photos uploaded to any server?
Absolutely not. All processing happens 100% locally in your browser's memory. We don't have a backend that stores or even sees your images.

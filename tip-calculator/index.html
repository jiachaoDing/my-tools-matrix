Tip Calculator FAQ

Is it better to tip on the total or pre-tax amount?
Standard etiquette suggests tipping on the pre-tax amount of your bill. However, many people find it simpler and more generous to tip on the total after-tax amount.
How does "splitting the bill" work?
Our calculator divides the total bill (including the tip) by the number of people you specify. This gives an equal amount for each person to pay.

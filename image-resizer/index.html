Image Resizer FAQ

What formats can I resize?
You can upload and resize JPEG, PNG, and WebP images. You can also convert between these formats during the resizing process.
How does percentage scaling work?
Percentage scaling adjusts the image dimensions relative to their original size. For example, 50% will make an image half as wide and half as tall as the original.
Is there a file size limit?
There is no hard limit, but performance depends on your device's memory. For extremely large images (40MP+), we recommend processing them in smaller batches.

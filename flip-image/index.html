Flip Image FAQ

What's the difference between horizontal and vertical flip?
A horizontal flip mirrors the image from left to right (useful for fixing mirror selfies). A vertical flip mirrors it from top to bottom (flipping it upside down).
Can I flip multiple images at once?
Yes! You can upload dozens of images at once and mirror them all with a single click. The tool processes them one by one in your browser.
Is there a file size limit?
No strict limit, but performance depends on your computer's memory. For very large images (4K+), we recommend processing them in smaller batches.

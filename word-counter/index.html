Common Questions

Is there a word limit?
No, there is no technical limit. You can paste an entire manuscript, and the tool will process it instantly using your browser's local resources.
How is reading time calculated?
We use an average reading speed of 200 words per minute (WPM), which is the standard benchmark for adult readers.
Is my text sent to a server?
Never. All text analysis happens locally in your browser. Your content is never uploaded, saved, or shared.

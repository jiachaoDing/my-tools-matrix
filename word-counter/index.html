FAQ

What is counted as a word?

Words are counted by splitting text on spaces, punctuation, and line breaks. Contractions like "don't" count as one word.

How are sentences counted?

Sentences are identified by periods (.), exclamation marks (!), and question marks (?) followed by whitespace or end of text.

Does it count special characters?

Yes, all characters including spaces, punctuation, and special symbols are included in the character count.

Is my text secure?

Absolutely. All text processing happens locally in your browser. Your content never gets uploaded to any server.

Can I count words in different languages?

Yes, the tool works with any language and character set, including non-Latin scripts like Chinese, Arabic, and Cyrillic.

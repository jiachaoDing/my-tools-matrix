Common Questions

What is WebP and why should I use it?
WebP is a modern image format that provides superior compression—typically 25-35% smaller files than JPG at the same quality. It supports transparency, lossy and lossless compression, and is now supported by 97% of browsers. Perfect for faster websites and apps.
Are my images secure during conversion?
Absolutely! All conversion happens locally in your browser using the Canvas API. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.
Can I convert WebP back to JPG or PNG?
Yes! This tool works both ways. You can convert JPG/PNG to WebP for smaller files, or convert WebP back to JPG/PNG for compatibility with older systems or applications that don't support WebP yet.
Is WebP supported by all browsers?
WebP is now supported by 97% of browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari (iOS 14+), and Opera. Only very old browsers don't support it, making WebP safe for modern web use.

Shopping FAQ

Is tax calculated before or after the discount?
In most regions, sales tax is applied to the final discounted price (the price you actually pay at the register). Our tool follows this standard logic: [Price - Discount] + [Tax on Discounted Price].
How do stacked discounts work?
Stacked discounts often apply sequentially. To calculate complex stacks, use our "Extra Off" field for fixed dollar amounts or subtract the final value once and run it again for the second percentage.

Photo Grid FAQ

What image formats are supported?
We support common image formats including JPEG, PNG, WebP, and GIF. For the best quality, we recommend using high-resolution source images.
Is there a limit on the number of photos?
No strict limit! You can create grids up to 10x10 (100 photos). However, processing many high-resolution photos may depend on your device's memory.
Are my photos uploaded to a server?
Absolutely not. All image processing happens locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your personal photos never leave your computer.

Expert Q&A

Are spaces counted as characters?
Yes, technically a space is a character. However, our tool provides two separate metrics: total characters (including spaces) and characters excluding spaces, so you have the exact data you need.
How do emoji affect the count?
Emojis are counted as single visual units in our character count, but they may consume more "bytes" depending on the platform's encoding. Our counter reflects the visual character count (length).
Is it safe to paste private text?
Absolutely. We use client-side JavaScript to process your text. This means your data never leaves your computer and is never sent to our servers.

Coin Flipper FAQ

How do you ensure the coin toss is fair?
We use the Web Crypto API (Specifically crypto.getRandomValues), which is designed for cryptographic security. It provides high-entropy random numbers that are far more unpredictable than standard Math.random().
Does it store my results?
No. All statistics and history are kept in your browser's memory for the current session only. Once you refresh or close the page, the data is gone. We do not store anything on our servers.
What is "Heads" and what is "Tails"?
Historically, "Heads" refers to the side of a coin with a portrait (head) of a monarch or leader. "Tails" is the opposite side. In our app, Heads is yellow/gold and Tails is silver/gray.

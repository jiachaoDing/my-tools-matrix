Frequently Asked Questions

When should I convert JPG to PNG?
Convert JPG to PNG when you need lossless quality, plan to edit the image further, require transparency support, or are working with graphics containing text or sharp edges. PNG is ideal for logos, screenshots, diagrams, and any image where quality preservation is more important than file size.
Will the PNG be larger than the original JPG?
Yes, PNG files are typically larger because they use lossless compression. While JPG sacrifices some quality for smaller file sizes, PNG preserves every detail. The size increase varies depending on image complexity—photos may be 2-5x larger, while graphics with solid colors may be similar in size.
Does converting JPG to PNG improve quality?
No, conversion cannot restore quality already lost in JPG compression. However, converting to PNG prevents further quality loss if you plan to edit the image multiple times. PNG's lossless format ensures that subsequent saves won't degrade quality, unlike JPG which loses quality with each save.
Is this converter really free with no limits?
Yes, completely free with zero restrictions. Since all processing happens in your browser, there are no server costs to pass on to users. You can convert unlimited images of any size, as many times as you need. No registration, subscriptions, or hidden fees—ever.

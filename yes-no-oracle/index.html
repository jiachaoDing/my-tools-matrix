The Way of Decision

The Zen Oracle is not just a random generator. It is a digital reflection of the I Ching (Book of Changes), designed to help you bypass the noise of the conscious mind and tap into a momentary state of clarity.

Understanding Yin & Yang

Every decision has two faces. Yang (Yes) represents the creative, the active, and the advancing force. Yin (No) represents the receptive, the yielding, and the protective force. The Oracle balances these energies to provide your answer.

The Hexagram Logic

While a simple Yes or No is provided, our engine generates a three-line Trigram (Bagua) for every consultation. This adds a layer of traditional symbolism to your modern decision-making process.

Common Questions

Is this based on authentic I Ching?
It uses the fundamental binary logic of the I Ching (Yin/Yang). While it doesn't provide full 64-hexagram readings, the underlying probabilities are weighted according to traditional concepts of Auspicious and Inauspicious movements.
How private is my question?
Completely. Your question is never sent to a server. Everything happens within your browser's memory. We don't even log it to a database—once you clear your history or refresh, it's gone forever.

Twitter Card FAQ

                    

                        

                            
                                What is "Summary with Large Image"?
                                
                                    
                                
                            
                            

                                This card type features a large, full-width prominent image along side a title and description. It's designed to give the reader a rich photo experience, and clicking the image will take the user to your website.
                            

                        

                        

                            
                                How do I test my Twitter Cards?
                                
                                    
                                
                            
                            

                                Once you've added the tags to your site, you can use the official Twitter Card Validator tool to preview your card and ensure all tags are working correctly.

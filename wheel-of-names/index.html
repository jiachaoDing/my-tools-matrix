Frequently Asked Questions

Is this wheel truly random?
Yes. We use the standard browser `Math.random()` engine, which is sufficient for non-critical random selections like giveaways and decision making.
Can I save my list?
Currently, we don't store your lists for privacy reasons. We recommend keeping a text file of your names and pasting them in when you're ready to spin.

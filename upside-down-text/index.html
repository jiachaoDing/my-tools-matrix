Upside Down FAQ

Where can I use flipped text?
You can use it almost anywhere that supports standard Unicode text! This includes Instagram, Facebook, Twitter (X), Discord, WhatsApp, and most modern messaging apps and website builders.
How does it work?
The tool maps each standard character to its upside-down Unicode equivalent. It's not a different font, but a collection of special characters that visually resemble your original text flipped 180 degrees.

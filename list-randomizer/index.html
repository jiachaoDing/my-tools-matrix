The Fisher-Yates Shuffle

Our randomizer uses the industry-standard Fisher-Yates algorithm, ensuring that every possible permutation of your list is equally likely. It's the gold standard for unbiased shuffling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to the list size?
Our tool can easily handle lists with thousands of items. The only limitation is your browser's memory, but for most everyday uses, it's effectively unlimited.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All shuffling happens locally on your device. Your list items are never uploaded to any server or stored anywhere outside your browser session.
